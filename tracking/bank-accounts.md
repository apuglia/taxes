## How to Download Statements

### Chase
1. Log in to chase.com
2. Click on the account
3. Go to "Statements"
4. Download each month as PDF

### Bank of America
1. Log in to bankofamerica.com
2. Click "Statements & Documents"
3. Select account and date range
4. Download as PDF

### Wells Fargo
1. Log in to wellsfargo.com
2. Go to "Statements & Documents"
3. Select the account
4. Download each statement

### Other Banks
Most banks have statements under "Documents", "Statements", or account settings.
Download ALL months for 2023 and 2024.
